## CrossbowMulticharge is ...
A _Minecraft Fabric 1.20.1_ mod that adds ability to charge into the crossbow **multiple arrows or fireworks**

## Basics of this mod
### How to charge crossbow?

If you try to charge crossbow, you may notice that crossbow behaviour is vanilla.
Now you need **sneaking** while *charging crossbow* to charge more than one arrow.<br>

### How many slots crossbow have?

Crossbow without '*Storage*' enchantment have **6** slots
